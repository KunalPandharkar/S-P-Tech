Our Services

Residential Interior Design

At Spaces & Places Interior Solutions, we create homes that reflect your personality and lifestyle. Our services include space planning for optimized flow, custom designs for living areas, furniture and decor selection, and complete project management through turnkey solutions. We also specialize in renovations and modern upgrades, and offer optional Vastu-based layouts for a balanced, positive space.

Commercial Interior Design

We know that commercial spaces need to be functional, on-brand, and inspiring. Our Commercial Interior Design Services include creating office interiors that enhance productivity, collaboration, and well-being, along with designing retail and showroom spaces that captivate customers. We also specialize in hospitality interiors, styling hotels, cafes, and restaurants for comfort and impact. Our space optimization services focus on improving efficiency, brand identity, and visitor flow. Additionally, we integrate your brand story into every aspect of the design, offering end-to-end execution from planning to sourcing, fit-outs, and final styling.

In-House Manufacturing & Production

At Spaces & Places Interior Solutions, we take pride in offering in-house manufacturing and production for our interior projects. This means that the furniture, cabinetry, modular kitchens, wardrobes, and custom-made elements for your home or office are made directly by our own team, under our direct supervision — not outsourced to third-party vendors.

Timely project completion & Handover

We understand that time is just as crucial as design when it comes to bringing your dream space to life. We recognize that a well-designed space is only truly valuable when it’s completed on time and with minimal disruption. That’s why we make timely project completion and smooth handover a top priority in every project we undertake. Our dedicated team works efficiently, managing each phase meticulously, ensuring deadlines are met without compromising on quality. We focus on delivering your vision within the agreed timeline, so you can enjoy your new space as soon as possible, with the least amount of hassle.
